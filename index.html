McDonald's

McDonald's, founded in 1940, is a global fast-food giant renowned for its consistent menu and quick service. With its iconic golden arches and offerings like the Big Mac and fries, it has become a symbol of fast-food culture worldwide. The company’s success lies in its ability to adapt to diverse markets while maintaining a recognizable brand identity. McDonald's also emphasizes innovation, with a focus on improving customer experience through technology and sustainability initiatives
